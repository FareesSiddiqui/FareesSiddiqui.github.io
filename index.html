Program Instructor

June 2019 - September 2019
Toronto Robotics Society, Toronto Ontario

At the Toronto Robotics Society, I was a program instructor that would teach children between the ages of 12 - 18 how to program robots for the 2019-2020 VEX robotics competition using C++.

During my time Voluteering at the Toronto Robotics Society, I gained an excess of 400 volunteering hours. My responsibilities were to maintain a healthy but competitive learning environment so that students could have fun and learn at the same time.

I developed a curriculum to aid in the learning process of the students. I taught them everything from basic C++, to advanced control theory such as PID, Odometry and Motion Profiling

About me

My name is Farees Ahmed Siddiqui, I am an aspiring computer scientest who resides in Toronto Ontario.

My hobbies include activities such as competitive programming, robotics and web development. I often enjoy competing in robotics tournements to further my knowledge about mechanical engineering and software development.

I really enjoy problem solving and wish to pursue a career which would let me do so for the rest of my life. Over the past 4 years I have competed in over 24 robotics tournements as well as 4 provincial championships and 2 world championships alongside my highschools robotics team.
